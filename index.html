The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
1. Set the final score to any number. If you don't set it, it is 100 by default.
2. Roll the dice as many time you wish. Press HOLD to save your score and to finish your round.
3. If you roll 1, your ROUND SCORE is nulled and it's the second player's turn.
4. If you roll two 6 in a row, your entire score is nulled and it's the second player's turn.
5. The first player to reach the final score wins the game.
